How It Works

Post your job. Review offers. Get it done!

1: Post Your Job

Describe your job and attach some pictures to explain what it is that you want done. Once your job has been posted, it’ll be live for providers to start bidding for it and applying.

2: Review Offers

You can review all the applications as they come in right from your dashboard, and can review provider's profiles, work histories, and ratings from previous jobs. Each applicant will provide their contact details (but they won't see yours unless you contact them), so it's up to you who you choose to connect with.

3: Get It Done

When your job has been finished and you’re happy with the result, all you need to do is mark the job as complete. That’s it! Job done! One more thing though; you’ll need to rate and review the quality of the job – this is to help the providers find other work and it's how maintain strict quality control on the platform.

Rating & Reviews

We encourage you to rate your providers once they’ve completed a job for you. This is to help them with finding future work and is a great way to highlight the things they did well, and also the things they could have done better. But don’t forget, it’s a two-way system and you’ll also get rated as a customer!
